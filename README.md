# MoodTunes - Your Daily Musical Mood Journal

## Description
MoodTunes is an open-source project that helps users track their daily moods and discover new music tailored to their emotional state. Users can log their mood for each day and the app will provide personalized music recommendations to match that mood.

## New Feature: Mood-Based Music Recommendations
One of the key features of MoodTunes is the ability to receive music recommendations based on your daily mood. Here's how it works:

1. **Log your Mood**: When you open the app, you'll be prompted to log your mood for the day on a scale from 1-10 (1 being the most negative, 10 being the most positive).
2. **Analyze your Mood**: The app will analyze your mood input and categorize it into one of several mood categories (e.g. happy, sad, angry, relaxed).
3. **Provide Recommendations**: Based on your mood category, the app will search a database of music tracks and provide a list of personalized recommendations that match your current emotional state. The recommendations will include a mix of popular and lesser-known tracks to help you discover new music.
4. **Save and Revisit**: You can save your daily mood logs and music recommendations, allowing you to revisit your past moods and rediscover the songs that resonated with you during those times.

This new mood-based music recommendation feature is a powerful addition to the MoodTunes app, helping users find music that truly aligns with their emotional state and enhancing their daily musical experience.
